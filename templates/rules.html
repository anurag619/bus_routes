Instructions for adding/editing a entry

1. There are 5 fields for every entry: start, end, bus number, bus type, via.

2. 'start' denotes starting of the respective bus

3. 'end' denotes the last stop of the respective bus

4. 'bus number' denotes the respective bus numer.

5. 'bus type' whether the bus is Private/Mini/Government.

6. 'via' denotes all the stops the respective bus passes.

7. Check if the respective entry is a new entry or an edit of the existing record.

8. Add as much as entry as possible, every record will be verified and then merged with the existing database.

9. Every week the original database will be updated once.
